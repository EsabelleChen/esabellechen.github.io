about me

I grew up in Montreal and Toronto and I am coming to design from a previous professional ballet career which aided in my developing an obsession with expression through creativity and movement. In starting my second career as a designer I chose to focus in branding because I have a passion for smart design that evokes an emotional response in its audience. I believe that design is essential in communication and emotional connection.
