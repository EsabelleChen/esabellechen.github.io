Gugu's Pantry

Gugu’s Pantry was born from the love of Taiwanese night markets and quintessential Taiwanese snacks. Inspired by the legendary “aunties” who always have a hidden stash of delicious treats and the nostalgia of late night snacks, we set out to share the vibrant flavors of Taiwan with Toronto.

In the bustling night markets of Taipei, where the air hums with the energy of street vendors and the laughter of friends sharing a bite. We explored hidden alleyways, unearthed generations-old recipes, and sourced the finest local ingredients.

Gugu’s Pantry is more than just snacks; it’s a bridge between cultures, a celebration of family traditions, and a love letter to the chaotic beauty of late night snacking. We believe that every bite is a journey transporting you to new and familiar places.
