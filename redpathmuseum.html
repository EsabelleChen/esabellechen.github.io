Branding - Redpath Museum

The Redpath Museum in Montreal, established in 1882, is a renowned natural history museum located on McGill University’s campus. With a striking stone building featuring Gothic Revival architecture, the museum houses over 1.3 million specimens including fossils, minerals, indigenous artifacts, and taxidermy animals. This cultural and educational hub offers visitors a unique opportunity to explore and appreciate the wonders of the natural world.

I created a new visual identity for Redpath Museum that I feel accurately reflects the museum’s rich history, diverse collection, and educational mission while also appealing to a broader audience. This rebrand strikes a balance between preserving the museum’s educational roots and presenting a modern, engaging, and inclusive image that attracts visitors of all ages and backgrounds.

The goal of this new look is to solidify The Redpath Museum as an industry leader in natural history as well as a beacon landmark of the beloved city of Montreal.
